Personal Profile

I am a doctoral researcher in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Siegen, Germany, working with the Ubiquitous Computing Group under Prof. Dr. Kristof Van Laerhoven.

My research focuses on environment-aware LoRaWAN communication for indoor sensing systems, with emphasis on path-loss modeling, reliability calibration, wearable and embedded sensor networks, and machine-learning-assisted propagation analysis. I am particularly interested in how wireless systems can adapt to changing environmental conditions and support robust, energy-efficient IoT deployments.
